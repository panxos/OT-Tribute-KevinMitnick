Kevin On Demand!

February 5-7, 1995


An important note for sessions 1-5 is that these are from before we had systematic analysis in place, so a lot of stuff was not being seen; it really depended on who was monitoring at the time. For example, the person monitoring session 4 (which happened during our first meeting with the Well folks on Monday night (6 Feb), didn't note the significance of the search strings being used to peruse various people's mail (such as "tsu" and "itni").

A lesson as to the importance of not just collecting data, but looking at it.
